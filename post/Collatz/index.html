The Collatz Conjecture

The Collatz conjecture is one of mathematics' most intriguing unsolved problems. Consider any positive integer \( n \):

Even Numbers

If \( n \) is even: \[ f(n) = \frac{n}{2} \]

Odd Numbers

If \( n \) is odd: \[ f(n) = 3n + 1 \]

The conjecture states that regardless of the starting number, this sequence will always reach 1. The CollatzR package provides tools to explore this sequence and its properties.
